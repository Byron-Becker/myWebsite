My journey into software engineering started out in healthcare when I was confronted with the challenge of training new therapists, and I envisioned a technological solution that could work alongside the new therapists to assist them. Although the concept was clear in my mind, bringing this idea to fruition proved challenging. This realization propelled me to delve into the world of software engineering, where I could give life to my idea.

The transition was a revelation, uncovering a profound passion for technological problem-solving. Now, as a software engineer, I am dedicated to transforming concepts into reality, assisting others in bringing their visions to life. I am eager to explore what I can develop with you.
